
= Registering a {mobile-client}

After provisioning Mobile Developer Console, the next step is to register the {short-mobile-client} that you are going to develop.

.Procedure

To create a {mobile-client}:

. Log into the OpenShift console.
. Choose project where you have previously deployed the Mobile Developer Console.
. Open the Mobile Developer Console by clicking on its route in the *Overview* screen.
. Log into the *Mobile Developer Console*.
. Click on `Create Mobile App` button.
. Enter a name for your {mobile-client}.
. Click `Create` button.
